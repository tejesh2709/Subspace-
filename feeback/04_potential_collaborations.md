# Feedback #4 — Potential Collaborations

## 🔴 Subspace is Sitting on a Distribution Goldmine It Hasn't Tapped

---

### (a) Observed

Subspace's subscription detection and saving features currently require users to download a standalone app. But the actual payment data Subspace needs already lives inside UPI apps — every recurring subscription charge, every auto-debit flows through PhonePe or GPay first.

- PhonePe processed **8.68 billion transactions in May 2025** — ~47% of all UPI volume in India
- PhonePe has **600 million+ registered users** and 40+ million merchants
- Every time a user's Netflix, Spotify, or Hotstar subscription auto-debits via UPI — that transaction goes through PhonePe or GPay

Yet there is **zero integration** between Subspace and India's dominant payment rails:
- No PhonePe mini-app
- No GPay plugin
- No "track this subscription" prompt after a recurring UPI debit
- No Account Aggregator integration with any major bank

---

### (b) Problem

Subspace's biggest acquisition challenge is convincing users to download *yet another* app to track money they're already tracking elsewhere. Over 72% of new Indian fintech product launches now involve at least one institutional partner (RBI Fintech Outlook 2026). Subspace is building its own distribution from scratch instead of riding rails that already reach 600 million Indians.

**The missed moment:**
Every time PhonePe processes a recurring Netflix debit, that's a perfect acquisition moment:
> *"You just paid ₹649 for Netflix. Get it for ₹149 via Subspace → Save ₹500/month"*

That moment currently goes to **nobody**. It's the highest-intent acquisition surface imaginable — a user who just felt the pain of paying full price — and Subspace isn't there.

---

### (c) Ship Instead

#### Option 1 — PhonePe Mini App ⭐ Highest Priority
A Subspace mini-app inside PhonePe's Switch ecosystem, surfaced automatically after every recurring UPI mandate processes.

- **Integration ask:** Post-payment nudge card with single CTA
- **Distribution:** 600M PhonePe users, zero additional CAC
- **Precedent:** Ola, Redbus, Swiggy already run mini-apps on PhonePe Switch

#### Option 2 — Account Aggregator Tie-up (ICICI / HDFC)
RBI's 2025 AA guidelines make bank-fintech API integration easier than ever. Subspace can pull subscription data directly from bank statements — far more complete than SMS/UPI parsing alone.

- **Benefit:** Closes the auto-detection gap (Feedback #3) at the source
- **Partners to approach:** Setu (AA gateway), Finvu, OneMoney

#### Option 3 — Corporate HR Platform Partnership
Target Darwinbox, Keka, or Razorpay Payroll. Pitch Subspace as an employee benefit.

- **Positioning:** "Help your team save ₹3,000/month on subscriptions"
- **Trigger:** Salary credit day — highest financial awareness moment of the month
- **Benefit:** Zero B2C CAC, high-intent users, recurring engagement tied to payroll cycle

---

### Partnership Priority Matrix

| Partner | Reach | CAC Impact | Effort | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| PhonePe Mini App | 600M users | Near-zero | Medium | 🔴 P1 |
| Account Aggregator (Setu/Finvu) | All bank users | Zero | Medium | 🔴 P1 |
| Darwinbox / Razorpay Payroll | 5M+ employees | Zero | Low | 🟡 P2 |
| Google Pay | 150M users | Near-zero | High | 🟡 P2 |

---

### Why This is the Highest-Priority Feedback

Subspace is bootstrapped with ₹36.5 Cr ARR and zero external funding. Traditional B2C marketing (paid ads, influencers) is expensive and doesn't compound. Partnership-driven distribution does. One PhonePe integration could deliver more qualified users in a month than a year of paid acquisition — at a fraction of the cost.
