# Feedback #1 — Competitor Analysis

## 🔴 OTT Account Sharing Crackdown Creates an Unhedged Business Risk

---

### (a) Observed

On the Google Play Store, Subspace has user reviews explicitly stating:
> *"I took a subscription to Amazon Prime — now the original subscriber logged me out and Subspace won't refund me."*

The app's core value proposition — up to 80% off on Netflix, Prime, Hotstar, YouTube Premium via shared accounts — depends entirely on OTT platforms tolerating multi-user account sharing.

There is **no mention anywhere** on the app or website of:
- What happens when a shared login stops working
- Any refund or compensation policy for broken access
- Whether listings are on official group plans or grey-area shared accounts

---

### (b) Problem

Netflix already cracked down on password sharing globally in 2023–24. Disney+ Hotstar and Amazon Prime are tightening device/profile limits in India. Subspace's highest-margin, most-marketed feature sits on a foundation that its competitors — **the OTT platforms themselves** — are actively dismantling.

This creates two compounding problems:

**1. User trust erosion**
Paying customers lose access mid-subscription with no recourse. This generates negative Play Store reviews, chargebacks, and word-of-mouth damage — exactly the opposite of what a bootstrapped, referral-dependent product needs.

**2. Business model fragility**
If 2–3 major OTT platforms enforce stricter sharing policies simultaneously, Subspace's subscription revenue takes a structural hit with no backup plan visible. Unlike CRED or Splitwise, Subspace's revenue is directly tied to the continuation of OTT platform tolerance — a policy it has zero control over.

---

### (c) Ship Instead

| Solution | What it does | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| **Sharing Guarantee** | Auto-credit user wallet within 24 hrs if shared login breaks mid-period | Low |
| **Pivot to official group plans** | Migrate inventory from grey-area accounts to platform-sanctioned family/group plans | Medium |
| **Trust badge on listings** | "Hosted on official group plan" label distinguishes safe listings from risky ones | Low |

**Tradeoff to consider:** The Sharing Guarantee costs money short-term but is essential for trust at scale. The pivot to official group plans may reduce margin per subscription but dramatically reduces churn and platform risk. Both are worth the cost.
